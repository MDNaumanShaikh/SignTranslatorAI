❓ How It Works

Quick guide to using SignFlow AI for recognition, translation, and sentence building.

1📸

Start Camera

Go to Live Recognition and click Start Camera. Grant browser permission. Ensure good lighting.

💡 Chrome / Edge recommended
2🖐️

Show a Sign

Hold one or both hands in frame (up to two). MediaPipe tracks 21 landmarks per hand; the model scores each hand and uses the stronger match.

💡 Hold steady ~1 second
3📝

Build Sentences

Each recognized sign adds to the Sentence Builder. Signs appear as colored word chips. Click a chip to remove it.

💡 Click 🔊 to hear the full sentence
4✨

Kinetic Typography

Words explode with per-letter animations. Each sign gets unique gradients and motion effects.

💡 Hover letters for interactive effects
5🔄

Text → Sign

Type any sentence in Text→Sign. Matching signs show as animated cards with emoji visuals.

💡 Matches gloss names from your vocabulary file
6📖

Dictionary

Browse and search all trained glosses. Cards show each label from your dataset.

💡 Great for learning new signs
7🤖

AI Coach

Interactive chatbot teaches signs with video tutorials and real-time practice feedback.

💡 Ask "Teach me THANK YOU" to start
